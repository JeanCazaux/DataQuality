## <img src='./logo.jpg' width='40' height='40'>FR Phone Number (parenthesis allowed)

### Overview
match French phone numbers in several format:
matches:
0033 1 47 25 00 00
+33 1 47 25 00 00
(33) 1 47 25 00 00
0033147250000
01-47-25-00-00
01 47 25 00 00
Does not match
0147 250 000
